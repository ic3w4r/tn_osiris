thesis:
TN-OSIRIS turns fragmented governance data into a Tamil Nadu command layer where districts, departments, projects, budgets, grievances, and operational flows become visible, comparable, and actionable.

audience:
government innovation stakeholders, platform sponsors, strategic partners, civic-tech investors, systems integrators

one-line arc:
Tamil Nadu governance is rich in signals but poor in visibility; TN-OSIRIS fuses a map-first command system with FlowRadar tracking so officials can see what is at risk, what is delayed, and what to act on next.
