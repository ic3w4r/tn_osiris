slide size: 1280x720
background system:
- primary: deep ink navy
- secondary: warm command paper
- selective accent panels in muted teal, amber, and rose

typography pair:
- titles: Aptos Display
- body and labels: Aptos
- mono / operational labels: Aptos Mono

color palette:
- ink: #06111e
- panel: #0e2036
- paper: #f4efe6
- paper-soft: #ece4d7
- teal: #27d3c3
- teal-soft: #183d46
- amber: #f0c955
- rose: #ff6e7c
- blue: #72a8ff
- slate text: #8ea2bc

chart grammar:
- authored with editable shapes
- direct labels preferred over legends
- high-contrast figures with compact support rails

diagram grammar:
- lanes and connectors must express data movement or operating responsibility
- rounded containers only when they denote modules or ownership groups

container / box grammar:
- 18-24px inner padding minimum
- low-contrast borders on dark slides, hairline separators on light slides

footer grammar:
- minimal source footer in low-contrast text
- TN-OSIRIS page marker at bottom right

title / kicker grammar:
- short uppercase kicker in mono
- strong claim title, never topic-only headings

allowed layout families:
- cover with metric rail
- editorial diagram
- product UI mock with side narrative
- module matrix
- role panels
- phased roadmap
- value matrix

banned motifs:
- generic feature-card grids
- heavy drop shadows
- decorative gradients without hierarchy value
- pseudo-government emblems
